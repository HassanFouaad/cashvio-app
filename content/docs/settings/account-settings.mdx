---

## Accessing Account Settings

1. Click on your **profile icon** at the top of the screen.
2. Select **Settings**, then navigate to **Account Settings**.

Or go to **Settings > Account Settings** from the sidebar.

## What You Can Configure

### Personal Information
- **First Name** and **Last Name** — Update your display name
- **Email** — Change your login email (requires verification)
- **Phone** — Update your phone number

### Changing Your Email

1. In Account Settings, click on the email field.
2. Enter your **new email address**.
3. Click **Save**.
4. A verification email is sent to your new email address.
5. Click the link in the verification email to confirm the change.

### Changing Your Password

1. In Account Settings, find the **Password** section.
2. Enter your **current password**.
3. Enter your **new password** and confirm it.
4. Click **Save**.

### Language Preference

Choose between **English** and **Arabic** (العربية). The interface switches immediately.

### Theme

Choose between **Light Mode** and **Dark Mode** for your interface appearance.

<Callout title="Note">
Account settings are personal — each user in your workspace has their own settings. Changing your language or theme does not affect other team members.
</Callout>
