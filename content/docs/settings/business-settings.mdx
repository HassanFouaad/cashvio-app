## Business Information

As the account owner (business owner), you can configure your business details:

1. Go to **Settings** in the sidebar.
2. Navigate to **Business Settings**.

### What You Can Configure

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Business Name** | Your official business name |
| **Tax ID** | Your tax registration number |
| **Business Address** | Your business's registered address |
| **Contact Information** | Business phone and email |

Click **Save Changes** after making updates.

## Subscription Plan

View your current subscription plan details:

1. Go to **Settings > Plan**.
2. You will see:
   - **Current Plan** — Which plan you are on
   - **Features** — What is included in your plan
   - **Usage** — How much of your plan limits you have used
   - **Upgrade Options** — Available plan upgrades

<Callout title="Note">
Business settings are only accessible to the account owner (business owner). Regular team members with roles cannot access this section.
</Callout>
