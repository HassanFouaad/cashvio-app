## How to Sign Up

To start using Cashvio, you need to create an account. Here is how:

1. Go to the Cashvio website and click **Sign Up** or **Get Started**.
2. Fill in the registration form with your details:
   - **Full Name** — Your first and last name.
   - **Email Address** — A valid email you have access to. This will be your login.
   - **Password** — Choose a strong password for your account.
3. Click **Create Account** to submit your registration.


## Logging In

Once your account is verified, you can log in:

1. Go to the Cashvio login page.
2. Enter your **Email** and **Password**.
3. Click **Login**.

You will be taken to your **Dashboard** — the main screen of Cashvio.

## Forgot Your Password?

If you forget your password:

1. On the login page, click **Forgot Password**.
2. Enter your **email address**.
3. Click **Submit**. A password reset link will be sent to your email.
4. Open the email and click the reset link.
5. Enter your **new password** and **confirm** it.
6. Click **Reset Password**.

You can now log in with your new password.

## Changing Your Language

Cashvio supports **English** and **Arabic**. To switch your language:

1. Look at the top of the screen for the **language selector**.
2. Click it and choose your preferred language (**English** or **العربية**).
3. The entire interface will switch to the selected language immediately.

<Callout title="Note">
The language setting applies to your session. Each user can choose their own preferred language independently.
</Callout>
