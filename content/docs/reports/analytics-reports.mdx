## Accessing Reports

Go to **Reports** in the sidebar. You will see several report categories, each accessible as a tab or sub-page.

## Sales Overview

The **Sales Overview** report shows your overall business performance:

- **Total Sales** — Revenue from completed orders
- **Orders Count** — Number of orders in the selected period
- **Average Order Value** — Total sales divided by order count
- **Growth Percentage** — Compared to the previous period

### Charts Available
- **Sales Trends** — Line chart showing sales over time (daily, weekly, monthly)
- **Sales by Store** — Bar chart comparing revenue across stores
- **Sales by Channel** — Pie chart showing which channels generate the most revenue
- **Sales by Payment Method** — Breakdown of how customers pay
- **Top Products** — Best-selling products by revenue or quantity

### Filters
- **Date Range** — Select the start and end date
- **View Period** — Day, Week, Month, or Year

## Profit Analysis

The **Profit** report helps you understand your margins:

- **Revenue vs. Cost** — Compare selling prices to cost prices
- **Profit Margins** — See your gross profit by product or category
- **Cost Analysis** — Track costs by supplier
- **Product Profitability** — Identify your most and least profitable products

<Callout title="Note">
Profit calculations use the **cost price** you set on each product variant. Make sure to keep cost prices up to date for accurate profit reporting.
</Callout>

## Customer Analytics

The **Customers** report shows customer behavior patterns:

- **Customer Acquisition** — How many new customers over time
- **Repeat Customer Rate** — Percentage of returning customers
- **Customer Lifetime Value** — Average total spending per customer
- **Customer Segmentation** — Grouping customers by spending patterns

## Staff Performance

The **Staff** report tracks team member metrics:

- **Sales by Staff** — Revenue generated per team member
- **Orders Processed** — Number of orders handled by each person
- **Performance Trends** — How individual performance changes over time

## Returns Analytics

The **Returns** report helps you understand return patterns:

- **Return Rate** — Percentage of orders with returns
- **Return Reasons** — Breakdown of why products are returned
- **Returns by Product** — Which products are returned most
- **Return Value** — Total monetary value of returns

## Business Insights

The **Insights** section provides data-driven recommendations for your business, helping you identify trends and opportunities.

## Exporting Reports

Most reports support **data export** for use in spreadsheets or other tools. Look for the export button on each report page.
